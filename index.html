About Mourad

Mourad Karami is a Public Figure and medical student at the Grigore T. Popa University of Medicine and Pharmacy. He is widely recognized for his digital initiatives to simplify complex medical concepts into accessible Moroccan Arabic (Darija). His work has been highlighted by major regional news outlets for its impact on youth health awareness.
