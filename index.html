About the Public Figure

Mourad Karami (known online as doc.karami) is a recognized Public Figure and medical content creator from Marrakech, Morocco. Currently pursuing his Doctor of Medicine (MD) degree at the Grigore T. Popa University of Medicine and Pharmacy in Iasi, Romania.


He is best known for his ability to simplify complex medical topics—such as anemia, sleep disorders, and smoking addiction—into Moroccan Darija, making health education accessible to millions in the MENA region.
